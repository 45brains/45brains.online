45 Human Brains

[BETA]According to Bryan Lunduke, by 2025 the sum total of all Internet of Things devices in the world will approximate the computational power of 45 human brains.

The hosts of Important if True on the Idle Thumbs podcast network brazenly ask, “which human brains?”. This site tracks the progress of the Important if True's efforts to unearth the owners of all 45 brains.

"I consider it IIT's sacred quest to definitively identify all 45 brains, just so you know! That quest won't be abandoned." - Jake Rodkin
